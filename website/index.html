The idea

A softer way to say hello.

Many social platforms are built around big events, endless feeds, or instant confidence. SofterHello is different: it starts with smaller gatherings, clearer context, and more room for quieter people to arrive as themselves.

The first pilot grows from North Shore Nights, a Sydney/North Shore alpha concept focused on calmer local meetups and thoughtful community design.

🌱

Small by design

Meetups should feel approachable, not overwhelming.

🧭

Know before you go

Arrival notes, group vibe, and expectations help reduce uncertainty.

🫶

Kindness over performance

The goal is genuine connection, not social status or self-promotion.
